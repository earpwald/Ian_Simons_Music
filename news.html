Music News

Ian and Ken plan to make 2014 a year for producing new material. We hope to provide scores of the songs along with lyric sheets and guitar chords.


Band News

The band has grown to become an 11 piece set up. This arrangement is ideal for large concerts but not so useful for general worship and praise gatherings. The wonderful thing about the members of the band is that we can now offer a variety of options for worship leading. The versatility of the mix enables us to perform as a 3 piece, 5 piece, 7 piece or when the occasion permits- 11 piece.

Hopefully with this set-up we can take our style of worship music to a wider audience and therefore develop our ministry through music.


Latest News

We plan to put some of our video footage onto YouTube. This will help us to share our music with a much wider audience than we could otherwise do with CD and live performances alone.

A few of the band members have started to write new material so hopefully we will be able to produce music in a variety of different styles - Our latest song 'Stay by my side' has pleased us very much. Hopefully we can record it in the not too distant future.


Good News

The Good News we want to promote is the Good News of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

All of our music is Christian in content and helping to lead God's people in worship is our desire.
